Questions

What format are the templates?
All graphics are Canva templates — click the link, duplicate to your account, swap in your name and colors. Takes under 2 minutes per post. No design experience required.
Do you cover US and Canadian markets?
Yes. Every caption set and stat reference comes in US and Canadian versions. LO content is compliant with NMLS advertising rules (US) and FSRA guidelines (Canada).
How is the Partnership Plan different from two individual plans?
The Partnership Plan includes everything in both individual plans PLUS 8 co-branded templates, a "Your Home Team" intro series, and RESPA/FINTRAC compliance guidance. Co-branded content is exclusive to this tier.
Is LO content compliance-safe?
All LO content avoids misleading rate claims and includes required disclosure fields. Co-marketing templates include RESPA cost-sharing guidance. Your compliance officer should still do a final review.
When does content drop each month?
The 1st of every month. You receive an email with your content calendar and all template links. Your first batch arrives within 24 hours of your subscription being confirmed.

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Founding member pricing is locked for life as long as you remain subscribed — if you cancel and resubscribe, standard pricing applies.

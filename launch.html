Instagram DMs
Highest conversion for this audience. Agents and LOs live on Instagram. Warm DMs to known contacts first, then cold outreach to agents posting 1-2x/week with under 5K followers — they feel the content pain acutely.
Target: 10 DMs/day · 20% response rate · 10% close rate
💼
LinkedIn
LOs and mortgage brokers are more active on LinkedIn than agents. Post the RESPA compliance angle — it has no competition in this space and is genuinely alarming to LOs who have not thought about it.
Target: 3 posts/week · 1 direct message per post engager
✉️
Email Cold Outreach
Pull agent emails from Zillow, Realtor.com, and local brokerage websites. LO emails from NMLS public registry. Subject line: "Free content templates for [MARKET] agents" converts well. 200 emails = 2-4 signups typically.
Target: 50 emails/day · 3% signup rate · 10% paid conversion
Where to Find Prospects
FINDING AGENTS
→ Instagram: Search #[yourcity]realtor, #[yourcity]realestate. Filter for 500-10K followers. Look for sporadic posting (1-2x/week) — they need you.
→ Zillow: Search your market, click agents, most have public emails
→ Local Facebook groups: "Real Estate Agents [City]"
→ Your personal network: every agent you have ever worked with
→ Your past clients who became agents
FINDING LOAN OFFICERS
→ NMLS Consumer Access (nmlsconsumeraccess.org): public registry with names and companies
→ LinkedIn: Search "Loan Officer [City]" — most have public profiles
→ Instagram: #[city]loanofficer, #mortgagebroker[city]
→ Local mortgage broker associations
→ Referral partners from your real estate career

I'm offering founding member pricing to the first group before the public launch — $49/month locked for life. Want me to send you the free starter pack?"

Your Monthly Rhythm
What happens every month.
  • 1st of the month: your new content pack email arrives in your inbox with the month's theme and a direct link to the generator
  • Open the generator, generate all 12 posts for the month (set market + tone once), done in 2 minutes
  • Post Monday, Wednesday, Friday every week — 12 posts covers the full month
  • Week 3 Friday: send the monthly market update email to your client list using the email template from the generator
  • Week 4 Friday: post the referral trigger — tag your agent or LO partner

For Loan Officers and Partnership Plans

Every LO and co-branded template includes NMLS number, state license, Equal Housing Lender language, and the RESPA §8 co-marketing disclosure pre-built. Fill in your credentials in the generator sidebar. Have your compliance officer or broker review each post before publishing. Your ContentVault subscription receipt documents fair-market-value cost-sharing for RESPA purposes — keep it on file.

Community
